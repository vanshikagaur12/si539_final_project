WorldLink

Revitalized a tech solutions firm’s website, boosting direct traffic by over 20% and decreasing primary user flow bounce rate by 30%.

Context

WorldLink, a tech solutions staffing firm, has experienced substantial growth in the past decade, however its website has not and remains unchanged from its last update in 2008. Its poor metrics and outdated branding underscore the pressing need for a revitalized online presence.

Problem

By looking at Google Analytics from the past 5 years, with an emphasis on the last 2 years, we learned that the current website had a very low percentage of return users (7.8%) and a high bounce rate (41.26%). In addition, the average session duration was less than a minute, leading to poor conversions.

Solution

We designed a brand new website from scratch laying emphasis on strong branding, clean UI, intuitive user flow, and authentic visuals consisting of WL employees. We also created a systems library to help with future rebrands and edits!
