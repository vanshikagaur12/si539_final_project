About Me

Hi, my name is Vanshika and I'm currently based in Ann Arbor, MI. I am pursuing a masters degree in Information Science with a UX and HCI specialization from the University of Michigan. I was previously Product Designer at WorldLink in Frisco, TX. I am passionate about creating accessible and inclusive experiences, that not only meet user needs but also predicts them.

Background

I graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a degree in Psychological Science which has been a significant source of inspiration for my work as it aids my understanding of human behavior. I am excited to bring my academic and practical experiences to my work as a Product designer.
